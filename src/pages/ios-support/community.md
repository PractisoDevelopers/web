# Joining the Community

The Practiso community is for publicly sharing questions and archives. On the Community page, you can search, preview, and download public archives from other users, or upload your own content.

## Browse Community Archives

The Community page shows a list of public archives. Each archive includes basic information and can be opened for a more detailed preview.

1. Open the Community page.
2. Browse the list of public archives, or use the search box to find content.
3. Tap an archive to open its preview page.
4. Review the questions and category content in the archive.

## Download Community Archives

1. Open the archive you need from the Community page.
2. Enter the archive preview page.
3. After confirming the content is suitable to import, choose download or import.
4. After download finishes, the archive content can be used in your local question bank or future practice.

If you only want to view the content, you can stay on the preview page without importing it immediately.

## Upload Content to the Community

You can upload content to the community from the Questions page or related sharing entry points.

1. Select the question or archive you want to share.
2. Open the share menu.
3. Choose to upload it to the community.
4. Follow the prompts to complete the upload.

Community archives are public by default. Before uploading, check question prompts, options, category names, and other information to avoid including content that should not be public.

## Manage Your Community Account

Tap the user button on the Community page to view current account information. The account page supports these actions:

- Change username.
- Change device name.
- Sign out.
- Deactivate account.
- Show credential QR code.

Your username identifies your public content in the community. The device name distinguishes different signed-in devices.

## Export and Import Sign-In Credentials

The show credentials feature exports the current account sign-in information as a QR code. You can import it with Practiso's scanning feature, or use another app to recognize the link and jump to import.

1. Tap the user button on the Community page.
2. Choose show credentials.
3. Scan the QR code on another device, or recognize the link in the QR code.
4. Follow the prompts to complete account import.

Keep the credential QR code safe. Anyone with the credentials may be able to sign in to the same community account.

## Deactivate Account

If you no longer use your community account, choose deactivate account on the account page. Before deactivating, make sure you understand the impact, such as no longer being able to use that account to upload or manage content.

## FAQ

### Are community archives public?

Yes. Archives uploaded to the community are publicly displayed, and other users can search, preview, and download them.

### Can I preview without downloading?

Yes. After entering the archive preview page, you can review the content before deciding whether to download or import it.

### How do I sign in to the same account after changing devices?

Show the credential QR code on the account page of the old device, then scan it or recognize the link on the new device to import the account.
