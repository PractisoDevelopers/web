# Privacy Policy for Practiso

Effective date: May 14, 2026  
Review date: May 14, 2027

Practiso ("Practiso," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy
Policy explains how Practiso collects, uses, discloses, stores, and protects
information when you use the Practiso app and related backend services.

By using Practiso, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

Practiso is designed to collect only the information needed to provide archive
upload, browsing, sharing, and account-related features.

### Information You Provide

We may collect the following information when you use the app:

- Client name and owner name, if you provide them.
- Uploaded Practiso archive files (`.psarchive`) and the content contained in
  those files.
- Archive names and metadata, including upload time, update time, dimensions,
  quiz counts, likes, and download counts.
- Actions you take in the app, such as uploading archives, liking archives,
  downloading archives, editing archive names, or deleting your account.

### Authentication Information

Practiso uses authentication tokens to identify your client and associated owner
account. These tokens are used to keep your account and uploaded archives
associated with you. We do not use these tokens for advertising or cross-app
tracking.

### Public Content

Uploaded archives are publicly accessible. This means that archive files,
archive names, owner names if provided, dimensions, preview information, likes,
download counts, and related metadata may be visible to other users or anyone
who can access the archive.

Do not upload archives that contain private, confidential, sensitive, or
personal information unless you are comfortable with that content being publicly
accessible.

### Information Collected Automatically by Infrastructure

Practiso is hosted using third-party cloud infrastructure. Standard server and
network systems may process technical information such as request times, URLs
requested, IP addresses, user agent strings, and diagnostic logs for security,
debugging, abuse prevention, performance monitoring, and service reliability.

Practiso does not use this information for advertising or tracking across apps
or websites.

## 2. Information We Do Not Collect

Based on the current Practiso app and backend implementation, Practiso does not
intentionally collect:

- Precise location data.
- Contacts.
- Photos or videos from your device library, except files you intentionally
  choose to upload on your own behalf.
- Camera or microphone data.
- Payment or purchase information.
- Advertising identifiers.
- Data for third-party advertising or cross-app tracking.

The app does not upload data to services other than the Practiso backend,
and uploads occur only when initiated by or on behalf of the user.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

- Provide, operate, and maintain Practiso.
- Store, host, display, and make publicly accessible uploaded archives and
  related metadata.
- Authenticate users and associate archives, likes, and settings with the
  correct account.
- Generate and display archive metadata such as dimensions, previews, likes,
  downloads, and timestamps.
- Process account and archive deletion requests.
- Protect the service from abuse, unauthorized access, and technical issues.
- Debug, monitor, and improve service reliability and performance.

## 4. Third-Party Services and Sharing

Practiso does not sell personal information. Practiso does not share information
for third-party advertising or cross-app tracking.

We use service providers to operate Practiso. These providers may process
information only as needed to provide infrastructure, storage, database,
hosting, diagnostics, or related service functionality.

Current backend-related service categories include:

- Cloud hosting and request processing through Cloudflare Workers.
- Object storage through Cloudflare R2 or compatible S3 storage.
- Database hosting through a PostgreSQL provider.
- Google Gemini, used to generate emojis for archive dimension names.

When Google Gemini is used, Practiso sends dimension names for emoji generation.
Practiso does not intentionally send full uploaded archive files to Google
Gemini for this feature.

We may also disclose information if required by law, legal process, or a valid
governmental request, or when necessary to protect Practiso, users, or the
public from harm, fraud, abuse, or security threats.

## 5. Public Sharing and Visibility

Uploaded archives are publicly accessible. Other users or visitors may be able
to access, download, preview, or view metadata for uploaded archives.

Publicly visible information may include:

- Archive content and files.
- Archive names.
- Owner names, if provided.
- Dimensions and generated emojis.
- Upload and update times.
- Like counts and some liker names when available.
- Download counts.

You are responsible for ensuring that you have the rights and permissions
necessary to upload and publicly share any archive content you submit to
Practiso.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain account information, uploaded archives, archive metadata, likes, and
related records for as long as needed to provide Practiso, comply with legal
obligations, resolve disputes, prevent abuse, and enforce our rights.

When you delete your account, Practiso attempts to delete associated client
records, owner records, archive metadata, likes, and stored archive objects from
the backend. Some residual information may remain for a limited time in backups,
logs, caches, or records maintained by infrastructure providers, where deletion
may not be immediate.

## 7. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access to, correction
of, deletion of, or information about the processing of your personal
information.

Practiso provides account deletion functionality through the backend. You may
also contact us at `practiso@icloud.com` to request assistance with
privacy-related questions or data requests.

Because uploaded archives are public, deleting an archive or account from
Practiso may not remove copies that others have already downloaded, cached,
saved, or shared outside Practiso.

## 8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information
processed by Practiso. However, no method of transmission or storage is
completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You should not upload sensitive information to Practiso, especially because
uploaded archives are publicly accessible.

## 9. Children's Privacy

Practiso is not intended for children under 13 years old, or the minimum age
required by applicable law in your jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect
personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal
information to Practiso, contact us at `practiso@icloud.com` and we will take
appropriate steps to review and delete the information where required.

## 10. International Processing

Practiso and its service providers may process and store information in
countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data
protection laws that differ from those in your jurisdiction.

## 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material
changes, we will update the effective date or otherwise provide notice as
appropriate. Continued use of Practiso after changes become effective means you
accept the updated Privacy Policy.

## 12. Contact Us

If you have questions, requests, or concerns about this Privacy Policy or
Practiso's privacy practices, contact us at:

`practiso@icloud.com`
